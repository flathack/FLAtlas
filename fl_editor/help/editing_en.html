Editing & Creation

Objects & Zones

  • Objects (bases, planets, suns, tradelanes, jump gates/holes, zones) can be selected by click and edited in the right panel.
  • Position, rotation, archetype, faction, loadout, and more fields are editable.
  • New objects are added via the right panel (creation). Placement is done by clicking on the map.
  • Zones (asteroids, nebulae, population) are shown as areas and can be adjusted in shape and size.

Complex Workflows

  • Bases: Complete workflow incl. rooms, market files, universe.ini entry, edit/delete.
  • Tradelanes: Create, edit, delete, reposition via two-click method.
  • Trade Routes: Central commodity-route management (create/edit/delete) including 2D route visualization across multiple systems.
  • Jump Gates/Holes: Connections between systems, incl. target selection and extra options.
  • IDS Management: Scan for missing names/info, CSV export, editing and reimport.

Undo & Save

  • All changes can be undone (Undo).
  • A backup of the original files is created before every save.
