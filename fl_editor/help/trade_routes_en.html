Trade Routes

The Trade Routes view manages commodity routes between bases centrally, separate from tradelanes (in-system flight lanes).

Features

  • Create/Edit/Delete: Manage routes from the left sidebar or via right-click in the table.
  • Filters: Commodity, minimum profit, search, and "same system only".
  • Sorting: All columns are sortable (including numeric values like price/profit/jumps).
  • 2D preview: Selecting a route automatically renders the route in the lower preview.

2D Visualization

  • Source and destination systems are shown in detail.
  • Intermediate transit systems are shown as points.
  • Route lines are red; local segments prefer tradelanes if they are faster/cheaper.
  • Environment zones (nebulae/asteroid fields) are overlaid as areas.
