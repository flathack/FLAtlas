Advanced Features

Multilingual & Themes

  • Switch between German and English at any time (toolbar).
  • Four themes: Founder, Dark, Light, Custom (your own accent color).
  • All translations customizable in translations.json.

INI Management & Exports

  • Automatic backups before every save.
  • Export and import of IDS CSV files for names and info.
  • Compatible with vanilla and mod setups.

3D View & Model Preview

  • Switch between 2D and 3D view.
  • 3D models (CMP/3DB) can be previewed directly.

Shortcuts & Controls

  • Esc: Cancel action
  • Mouse wheel: Zoom
  • Ctrl+Mouse wheel: Height in 3D
